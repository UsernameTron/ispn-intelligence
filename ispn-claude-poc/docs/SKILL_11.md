# M&A Integration Intelligence

## Overview

Enterprise M&A integration management system designed for organizations executing multiple simultaneous acquisitions with complex synergy targets, customer retention requirements, and PE investor oversight. Transforms fragmented integration data across projects, financial systems, and team communications into unified M&A command center with real-time synergy tracking, risk identification, and board-ready integration reporting.

**Primary Use:** Multi-acquisition integration oversight, synergy realization tracking, integration playbook optimization, board/investor M&A reporting
**Key Value:** Reduces integration management from 8 hours/week to 30 minutes/week while improving customer retention to 100% and synergy realization to 90%+
**Annual Impact:** $800,000+ in integration efficiency, synergy acceleration, and deal capacity expansion

## When to Use This Skill

Use for:
- Weekly M&A integration dashboard for CEO and leadership reviews
- Real-time synergy tracking (revenue and cost synergies actual vs. target)
- Customer retention monitoring across acquisitions (target: 100%)
- Integration playbook optimization and best practice identification
- Board/investor M&A performance reporting
- Integration risk identification and mitigation
- Deal capacity assessment for future acquisitions
- Integration team coordination and resource allocation

**Typical Request:** "Show me integration status for iGLASS and DxTEL. Include synergy realization actual vs. target, customer retention, team integration progress, systems migration status, and board reporting summary. Flag anything requiring CEO attention."

**Revenue Synergies Monitored:**
- Cross-sell to existing customer base ($ realized and pipeline)

- New market access and geographic expansion
- Product/service bundling opportunities

**Cost Synergies Monitored:**
- Systems consolidation savings (CRM, ERP, infrastructure)
- Facility optimization and real estate savings
- Purchasing leverage and vendor consolidation
- Operational efficiency improvements
- Overhead reduction and span of control optimization

**Synergy Acceleration:**

**What Claude Monitors:**
