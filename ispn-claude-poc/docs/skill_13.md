## Best Practices for E-rate Cycles

### Q1 (January-March): E-rate Planning & Filing Season

**Focus:** Support customers with E-rate applications, maximize early pipeline visibility

**Key Activities:**
- **E-rate Application Support:** Help customers with Form 470 filing (needs assessment, competitive bidding)
- **Pipeline Building:** Respond to Form 470 RFPs, submit competitive proposals
- **Forecasting:** Build Q2-Q3 pipeline based on E-rate application tracking
- **Content Marketing:** Publish E-rate optimization guides, webinars, checklists

**Claude Use Cases:**
- Automated RFP response generation (high volume of Form 470 responses)
- E-rate pricing calculator proposals (accurate discount rate applications)
- Nurture campaigns to districts approaching application deadlines
- Competitive analysis for pricing optimization (win rate improvement)

### Q2 (April-June): E-rate Award & Closing Season

**Focus:** Close deals aligned with E-rate award notifications, maximize Q2 revenue

**Key Activities:**
- **Deal Velocity:** Accelerate contracts following E-rate commitment letters
- **Implementation Planning:** Schedule installations for summer break (minimal disruption)
- **Renewal Focus:** Engage customers with June-August expirations (secure renewals early)
- **Expansion Opportunities:** Upsell managed services, WiFi upgrades alongside connectivity deals

**Claude Use Cases:**
- Customer health monitoring (identify at-risk renewals proactively)
- Automated renewal proposal generation (high volume of expirations)
- Deal coaching for competitive situations (win deal vs. lose to competitor)
- Content creation showcasing Q1 wins (momentum for Q3 pipeline building)

### Q3 (July-September): Summer Deployment & Pipeline Rebuilding

**Focus:** Execute summer installations, rebuild pipeline for Q4/Q1

**Key Activities:**
- **Implementation Execution:** Install and cutover deals closed in Q2
- **Customer Success:** Ensure smooth launches, address post-deployment issues
- **Pipeline Development:** Build Q4/Q1 pipeline (early renewals, expansion deals)
- **Conference Season:** Attend state education conferences, generate new leads

**Claude Use Cases:**
- Account-based marketing for conference attendees (personalized follow-up)
- Case study creation highlighting summer deployment successes
- Expansion opportunity identification (proactive CSM outreach)
- Sales coaching for rebuilding pipeline (non-E-rate opportunities)

### Q4 (October-December): Year-End Planning & Early E-rate Prep

**Focus:** Close year-end budget flush deals, prepare for Q1 E-rate season

**Key Activities:**
- **Budget Flush Opportunities:** Target districts with unused capital budget (use-it-or-lose-it)
- **Early Renewals:** Secure commitments for Q1 expirations (reduce Q1 risk)
- **Annual Planning:** Build FY revenue plan, sales territories, quota allocation
- **E-rate Preparation:** Pre-RFP discussions with key targets (build relationships before Form 470)

**Claude Use Cases:**
- Revenue forecasting for annual planning (historical patterns + E-rate intelligence)
