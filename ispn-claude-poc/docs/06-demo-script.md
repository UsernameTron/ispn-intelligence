# Facilitated Demonstration Script: Claude Operational Intelligence Platform
## Charlie Brenneman - Senior Vice President, Operations & Strategic Initiatives

**Demo Duration:** 45-60 minutes (including 15-minute Q&A)

**Target Audience:** Brenneman + CEO + Board Member (3-5 people)

**Objective:** Demonstrate how Claude transforms operational visibility and strategic initiative management from fragmented, manual process to unified, automated intelligence platform

**Demo Success Criteria:**
- Audience understands problem (fragmented operational data, manual synthesis consuming 30+ hours weekly)
- Audience sees solution in action (unified briefing, scenario modeling, dependency mapping)
- Audience believes ROI and implementation timeline
- Audience commits to pilot (or schedules follow-up decision meeting)

## Demo Success Metrics

After demo concludes, measure success:

1. **Audience Reaction:** Did they understand the problem and solution? (Look for nods, positive comments)

2. **Questions Quality:** Were questions substantive and positive? (Good sign: "How do we scale?" vs. "Why would this work?")

3. **Commitment Level:** What's the next step? (Best: approval to move forward | Good: follow-up meeting to discuss | Concerning: "Let us think about it")

4. **Individual Feedback:**
   - Brenneman: "This solves my exact problem"
   - CEO: "This accelerates our strategic velocity"
   - Board Member: "This strengthens our governance"

5. **Decision Timeline:** When will decision be made? (Best: today | Good: this week | Concerning: undefined)

**Demo Complete When:**
- Audience clearly understands problem, solution, and value
- ROI case is compelling and credible
- Implementation timeline is clear and achievable
- Audience commits to next step (approval or follow-up discussion)
