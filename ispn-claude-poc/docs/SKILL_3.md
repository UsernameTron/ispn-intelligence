# Compliance & Audit Readiness

## Overview

Continuous audit readiness system for PE-backed multi-acquisition organizations maintaining SOX-compliant controls, documentation automation, and audit preparation. Designed for CFOs managing external audits, PE investor due diligence, and regulatory compliance across multiple acquired entities.

**Primary Use:** Audit preparation, compliance monitoring, control testing, documentation management, PE investor reporting
**Key Value:** Reduces audit prep from 120 hours to 30 hours while maintaining continuous compliance readiness
**Annual Impact:** $150,000+ in CFO/Controller time savings and audit efficiency

- Annual external audit preparation (typically Q1)
- Quarterly reviews for PE investors
- SOX compliance control testing (quarterly)
- M&A due diligence documentation
- Regulatory compliance monitoring
- Internal audit support
- Board audit committee reporting

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Audit Documentation Automation

**What Claude Automates:**
- Control testing documentation (SOX controls, financial reporting controls)
- Financial close workpapers (reconciliations, journal entries, variance analysis)
- Entity-level financial schedules (ISPN, iGLASS, DxTEL separate + consolidated)

**Risk Areas Tracked:**

**Quarterly Control Testing:**
- Preventive controls: Authorization, approval workflows

- Entity-level controls: Tone at top, financial oversight

**Testing Documentation:**
